About Me


I am currently a Junior at the University of Washington's Information School, specializing in Data Science. The Informatics program at the Information School offers me a plethora of skills such as: programming, designing, project management, networking, database management, and web development; similar to a Computer Science program but with a Human and Team-Centered approach. Through my coursework I have experience with both the programming, designing, and research aspects in developing websites and mobile applications.

I have always developed a passion for tech; but also for music, sports and video games. Whether it is with my bass guitar or with my ukelele, in my free time I try to relax by playing some music and learning new songs. Coming from the island of Puerto Rico, baseball was my favorite sport growing up; but then I found a love for basketball, tennis, soccer and most recently, Football and Hockey. My favorite teams are the Seahawks, the Mariners, the Sounders and the Washington Huskies. I enjoy traveling, and my goal is to travel more around the world.
